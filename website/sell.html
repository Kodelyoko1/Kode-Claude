FAQ

Honest answers.

Is this really free?

Yes — completely free for sellers, forever. We make our money from investors who pay a monthly subscription to access the list. You'll never be charged a fee or commission for being on it.

Will I definitely get an offer?

No guarantees. Most properties get 2–5 inquiries within the first week of being on the list. Distressed, discounted, or unusually motivated situations tend to get more. Move-in-ready houses at retail price get fewer (investors look for deals).

How much will buyers offer?

Cash buyers typically offer 60–80% of after-repair value. The exact number depends on condition, location, and how motivated they are. It'll usually be less than retail (an agent listing) but more than foreclosure, and the close is fast.

Am I obligated to accept any offer?

Never. You're a free agent. Take the best offer, counter, or walk away from all of them. We're not in the contract — you negotiate directly with whichever buyer you choose.

I'm behind on payments — can buyers still help?

Often yes. Pre-foreclosure properties tend to attract the most attention from our cash buyers because they can close in 14–21 days, beating a trustee sale. Some buyers will even work directly with your lender on the payoff.

Who actually buys the property?

One of the investors on our list — a real cash buyer who calls you, makes an offer, signs a contract with you, and closes through a title company. Wholesale Omniverse LLC is not the buyer; we connect you with buyers.

What about my privacy?

Your property details, name, phone, and (if provided) email go to paying subscribers — typically 100+ active investors. They'll contact you directly. If you don't want your phone shared, this isn't the right channel for you. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
