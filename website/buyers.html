FAQ

Honest answers.

How many deals will I see per month?

Volume varies by market and your buy box width. Active markets like Detroit, Memphis, and Atlanta typically see 4–10 deals/month matching common buy boxes. Narrower markets or unusual criteria see fewer.

What if no deals fit my buy box in a given month?

You can pause your subscription anytime via PayPal. Or update your buy box to widen the filter. We'd rather you pause than pay for nothing.

How do I claim a deal?

Reply to the deal packet email or text the number on the packet. First reply with funds verification gets the contract assigned. We don't run auctions — it's first come, first served.

What's the assignment fee?

Varies per deal but typically $5,000–$15,000. Listed clearly in every packet. You see the assignment price upfront — no hidden math.

Do you do JVs or splits?

Occasionally on bigger deals. Reach out directly if you want to talk JV.

What if I want to cancel?

One click in PayPal. We won't email you back asking why or trying to win you back.
