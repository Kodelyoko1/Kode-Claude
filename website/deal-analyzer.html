What it does

Everything you need to decide on a deal — in one run.

Live comp pull

Pulls comparable sales from Redfin’s bounding-box API — same data your buyers use. Filtered by bed/bath/sqft/radius. No API key, no account required.

ARV calculation

Weighted median of the closest comps (most recent sales weighted highest). Gives you a defensible ARV number to show buyers — not a guess.

MAO calculation

Max Allowable Offer = (ARV × 70%) − Repairs − Holding − Assignment Fee. Adjustable thresholds. Flags deals where margin is too thin before you waste time on them.

LOI generation

Generates a ready-to-send Letter of Intent with the offer price, subject-to clause, inspection period, and closing timeline pre-filled. Copy-paste straight to email.

Seller email drafts

AI drafts a follow-up email to the seller, tailored to the deal situation (low equity, distress signal, inherited property). You review and send, or it goes out automatically in —auto mode.

Autonomous mode

Run with --auto and it processes your entire lead queue overnight — analyzes every deal, drafts every LOI, sends follow-up emails. Wake up to a done inbox.

FAQ

Honest answers.

How is this different from PropStream or DealMachine?

Those are list-building tools — they help you find properties. The Deal Analyzer runs the math after you have an address: comps, ARV, repair estimate, MAO, and a ready-to-send LOI. It works alongside whatever lead source you use, not instead of it.

Does it work for any market?

Any U.S. market covered by Redfin. Redfin covers most metros and many rural areas. If your market doesn’t have enough comp data, the analyzer flags it so you know not to rely on the ARV.

Do I need coding experience to use it?

No. You run one command: python3 main.py for interactive mode or python3 main.py --auto for batch mode. You’ll need Python 3 installed (free, 5-minute install). We walk you through setup on your onboarding call.

What’s the 70% rule and can I change it?

The 70% rule (MAO = ARV × 0.70 − repairs) is the default. You can adjust the multiplier in your config file. Want to target 65% deals in a hot market? Change one number. The formula is fully transparent and yours to tune.
