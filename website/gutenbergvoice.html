Frequently Asked Questions

Are the texts really copyright-free?

Project Gutenberg only hosts works in the public domain — typically books published before 1928 in the US. The scripts GutenbergVoice generates are also yours to use commercially, sell as audiobooks, or license.

Can I request a specific Gutenberg book?

Yes. Email WholesaleOmniverse@gmail.com with the Gutenberg book number (found in the URL) and the plan you've purchased. Full processing typically takes under 5 minutes for chapter packs.

Does this work with AI narration tools like ElevenLabs?

The scripts are formatted specifically for AI narration workflows — narrator and character cues are labeled, dialog is separated, and pause markers are included. You can paste directly into an ElevenLabs voice project.

What is the "character voice sheet" in the Full Kit?

A separate file listing every named character in the book along with voice direction notes (age, class, accent suggestions, emotional register). Makes casting AI voices or human voice actors significantly faster.
