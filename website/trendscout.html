Questions

How is TrendScout different from Google Trends?＋

Google Trends shows raw volume — TrendScout shows ranked, filtered, monetizable niches. We block noise categories, compare week-over-week, and surface only signals that fit a digital-product creator's workflow. It's an editorial filter on top of keyword data, not raw data.

What's in the "noise filter"?＋

Crypto/NFT, celebrity events, supplements with FDA-adjacent claims, political topics, gambling, and pure seasonal spikes (e.g., "Valentine's Day cards" in February). We also filter niches where the top sellers have 10k+ reviews — too saturated for a new creator to break in.
