Frequently Asked Questions

What software do I need to open the output PDF?

Any PDF reader — Adobe Acrobat, Preview on Mac, or Chrome's built-in PDF viewer. The file is also compatible with all print-on-demand services (Printify, Printful, Lulu) and any home printer.

Can I sell the planners I generate?

Yes, the generated PDFs are yours to sell commercially. Many customers upload directly to Gumroad or Etsy. There are no royalties or licensing restrictions on the output.

Can I include my own logo or brand name?

Yes. Add a "brand" key to your manifest with your business name — it appears in the page header and cover page. Logo image support is available on the Monthly and Unlimited plans.

What's the difference between Habit Tracker and Goals Journal?

Habit Tracker is a grid layout — rows are habits, columns are days. You check off each day. Goals Journal is more narrative — quarterly objective, monthly milestones, weekly focus, and reflection prompts. Choose based on whether you want a checkbox system or a writing system.
