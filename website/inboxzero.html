Classification rules

⚡

Urgent

Account alerts, payment failures, verification requests, legal notices, time-sensitive keywords ("urgent", "action required", "expires today").

📌

Important

Direct messages from real people with no promotional headers, invoice/contract attachments, replies to threads you initiated.

📰

Archived (newsletter)

Any email with List-Unsubscribe header, Mailchimp/Klaviyo/Beehiiv sender patterns, or "weekly digest" subject keywords.

🛍️

Archived (promo)

Discount codes, "sale ends", "limited time", "% off" — even if the subject sounds urgent. Promo mass-mail headers always override urgency classification.

👥

Archived (social)

LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook notification emails. Anything from noreply@ addresses at major social platforms.

🤖

Claude Escalation (opt-in)

Ambiguous "other" messages can be escalated to Claude for a second-opinion classification. Requires an Anthropic API key — completely optional.

Questions

Why App Password instead of OAuth?＋

OAuth requires registering a Google Cloud app and going through Google's verification process, which takes weeks. A Gmail App Password is a 16-character IMAP token you generate in 2 minutes from myaccount.google.com. It gives IMAP access to your inbox — nothing else. You can revoke it instantly at any time.

Does it delete anything?＋

No. It only archives. Everything that gets triaged is still in your Gmail Archive tab — accessible any time with a search. We never delete emails.

What if something urgent gets archived incorrectly?＋

It's in Archive, not Trash. You can find it instantly via Gmail search. Email us with the false-positive pattern and we'll add it to your custom exclusion list — no extra charge.

Does it work with Google Workspace / business Gmail?＋

Yes, as long as your Workspace admin has IMAP enabled for your account. Most do by default. You generate an App Password the same way as a personal Gmail.

How do I cancel?＋

Email WholesaleOmniverse@gmail.com. Your subscription is cancelled immediately. You can also revoke your App Password from Google's security settings at any time to instantly disconnect IMAP access.
