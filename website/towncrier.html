Questions

How do events get added to the digest?＋

You (or we) drop a saved HTML snapshot of your city's local event pages — municipal calendars, Facebook events pages, local news event listings — into the input folder. TownCrier parses each snapshot, extracts event names, dates, times, and locations, then classifies each listing and builds the digest automatically. No API keys required for the scraping step.

What cities are supported?＋

Any city where you can save a local HTML event listing page. The agent is source-agnostic — it parses the HTML you provide, so if your city has a public events calendar, a local Facebook group, or a community events page, it works. We currently run digests for cities in Maine, New Hampshire, Vermont, and Massachusetts, but can onboard any US city within 24 hours.

How does sponsor billing work?＋

Sponsor slots are one-time purchases per issue. Email WholesaleOmniverse@gmail.com with your business name, slot preference (header/mid/footer), and the issue date you want. We'll send a PayPal invoice. Once paid, your placement is locked into that week's digest and goes out with the subscriber send. Featured event listings work the same way — pay $25, send us your event details, and it's pinned in the next issue.

Can I white-label this for a local business client?＋

Yes. The newsletter digest can be branded with any business name, logo, and color scheme. Local media companies, chambers of commerce, and community orgs have used TownCrier under their own brand to grow their subscriber lists and sell sponsorships to local advertisers. Email us to discuss white-label pricing — typically a flat setup fee plus the standard $97/mo service rate.
