FAQ

Common questions

How do I add leads to the sequence?
Add a record to data/leads.json with the seller's name, email, address, and the date you first contacted them. The agent calculates all 6 send dates from that initial contact date and queues them automatically. New leads added mid-month start their own 60-day clock.
What if the seller replies before the next scheduled touch?
A reply flags the lead as hot in leads.json. The sequence automatically pauses for that lead — you take over the conversation. No awkward automated email firing into an active negotiation.
Can I customize the email templates?
Yes. The templates live in followup_agent/tools.py as the TEMPLATES dict — one entry per touch. Edit the subject line and body for any touch and the next run picks up the changes. Keep the tone conversational — that's what gets replies.
Does it work with the Deal Analyzer?
Yes — both agents share data/leads.json. When a hot lead responds and you're ready to make an offer, run the Deal Analyzer on that lead's address: it pulls comps, calculates your MAO, and can draft an LOI email to send directly to the seller.
