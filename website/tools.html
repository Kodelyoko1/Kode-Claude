FAQ

Honest answers.

How is the Deal Analyzer different from PropStream or DealMachine?

Those are list-builders. The Deal Analyzer runs the math AFTER you have an address: comps, ARV, repair estimate, MAO, and the LOI text. It pairs with whatever list source you already use.

What markets does Outreach-as-a-Service cover?

Any US market with accessible county property records. Detroit, Memphis, Atlanta, Cleveland, Chicago, Kansas City, Norfolk are our most-active. Smaller markets work too — just need decent open data.

Do you guarantee leads?

No. Anyone guaranteeing motivated seller leads is lying. We guarantee the volume of outreach we promise per tier — typically 800–3,000 contacts/month depending on plan. Hot-reply rates run 1–4% in good markets.

Can I see a sample lead report before signing up?

Yes — email me with your target market and I'll send a sample report from a recent campaign in a similar city.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Month-to-month, no contracts. Cancel via reply email — we don't make you call to leave.
