Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Wholesale Omniverse LLC ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you visit wholesaleomniverse.com or interact with our services. We wrote this in plain English. If anything's unclear, email WholesaleOmniverse@gmail.com.

1. What we collect

We only collect information that you give us directly or that we need to run our services.

  • Seller intake form: property address, city, state, ZIP, your name, phone number, email (optional), reason for selling, condition, and timeline.
  • Cash buyer / wholesaler signups: name, email, phone, business name, target markets, buy box, payment method (handled by PayPal — we never see your card details).
  • Email replies and call/text exchanges: any information you voluntarily share during follow-up.
  • Technical info: your IP address and browser user-agent when you submit a form (used only to detect spam and abuse).

We do not use third-party analytics scripts, ad pixels, or tracking cookies on this website.

2. How we use it

  • To prepare a cash offer for your property and contact you about it.
  • To follow up if you don't respond right away (our email sequence runs at days 3, 7, 14, 21, 30, and 60 — you can stop it any time by replying "remove" or "stop").
  • To send deal packets to subscribers on our Cash Buyers list.
  • To deliver the services you've paid for (Deal Analyzer, Outreach-as-a-Service).
  • To respond to your questions and support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Who we share it with

We do not sell your information to third parties. The only times we share data are:

  • Cash buyer subscribers (the core of our service): When you submit your property through our seller form, your property details and contact information become part of the weekly property list sent to paying cash-buyer subscribers (typically 100+ active investors). They use this info to contact you directly with cash offers. This is what the service does — by submitting the form you're consenting to this use. If you do not want your information shared with subscribers, do not submit the form.
  • Occasional wholesale assignments: Wholesale Omniverse LLC may also pursue a wholesale contract on a property submitted through our seller form. In that case, we would make you a written offer, sign a purchase agreement with you, and then assign that contract to an investor in our buyer network who closes the purchase. The agreed-upon price you receive at closing remains the same; we earn an assignment fee from the end buyer. This is standard wholesale real estate practice and would be fully disclosed in any contract we sign with you.
  • Service providers we rely on: Gmail/Google (for sending email), PayPal (for processing payments), and our hosting provider (Netlify). These companies have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data on our behalf.
  • Legal requirements: If we receive a valid subpoena, court order, or law enforcement request, we comply as required by law.

4. Communications consent

When you submit the seller intake form, you consent to being contacted by phone call, SMS text message, and email about your property and our services. Standard message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any text message we send
  • Replying UNSUBSCRIBE or REMOVE to any email we send
  • Emailing WholesaleOmniverse@gmail.com and asking us to remove you

5. Data retention

We keep submitted information for as long as needed to provide our services and follow up on potential deals, and to comply with our legal and accounting obligations. If you ask us to delete your record, we will do so within 30 days unless we're required by law to keep it longer.

6. Security

Your information is stored on servers we operate. We use industry-standard practices — encryption in transit (HTTPS), restricted access, and regular software updates — to protect it. No internet-based service can promise 100% security, but we take reasonable steps to safeguard what you share with us.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we have about you
  • Request a copy of that information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information (with certain legal exceptions)
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, email WholesaleOmniverse@gmail.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

8. Children

Our services are not directed at anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, email us and we'll delete it promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we'll post the new version here with a revised "Last updated" date. Material changes will be noted at the top of the page.
