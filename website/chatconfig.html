How It Works

Three steps from FAQ list to live bot

No chatbot builder logins. No drag-and-drop flow editors. Just a JSON file and a script run.

1

Drop Your FAQ Manifest

Create a data/cc_inputs/{slug}.json with your business name, hours, contact info, and an array of FAQ objects — each with a question and answer.

2

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a Voiceflow or Botpress account?
Only if you want to use those platforms — and they both have free tiers that are more than enough for a small-business FAQ bot. The simple_responses.json export works with Drift, Intercom, or any custom widget without any third-party account at all.
How many FAQs can I include?
The One-Time Setup supports up to 20 FAQ pairs. The Multi-Bot plan supports up to 30 per bot. Each FAQ is auto-expanded into multiple trigger phrases, so 20 FAQs typically covers 100+ real-world question variations your customers might type.
Can the bot handle questions outside the FAQ list?
The bot is trained only on what you provide — it does not hallucinate answers. For questions it does not recognize, it falls back to a configurable escalation response (e.g. "Let me connect you with a team member") using the escalation field in your manifest.
How do I update the FAQ later?
Edit your manifest JSON, drop it back into data/cc_inputs/, and run the agent again. The full 4-file bundle is regenerated in seconds. Monthly Maintenance subscribers send their updated manifest and we handle the re-run and re-import for them.
