FAQ

Common questions

What genres are supported?
Thriller, literary fiction, fantasy, science fiction, romance, horror, cozy mystery, historical fiction, and memoir. If your WIP is genre-blended — say, a sci-fi thriller with romance subplots — just tell us the primary genre and the blend when you set up. Prompts will reflect the mix.
What's in a story bible?
The story bible is a structured reference document covering: full character dossiers (name, background, arc, voice notes, relationships), a chapter-by-chapter outline, a three-act plot breakdown with key turning points, a world-building guide (rules, geography, history where applicable), and thematic throughlines so your motifs stay consistent from chapter one to the last page. It arrives as a formatted markdown file you can drop straight into Notion or Google Docs.
How does streak tracking work?
Reply to any daily prompt email with your word count for that session — even just "wrote 300 words today" is enough. StoryForge logs the entry, increments your streak counter, and includes your running streak total in each morning's prompt. If you miss a day, the streak resets to zero and a recovery prompt lands the next morning to get you back on track. Your all-time longest streak is always preserved.
Can I change my genre mid-month?
Yes — email any time and the genre switch takes effect on the next prompt. If you're mid-draft on a thriller but want to test romance prompts for a new project, just say so. You can also run two genres simultaneously if you're drafting more than one project.
