The 70% Rule (and why real wholesalers go deeper)

MAO = (ARV × Buyer's margin %) − Repairs − Holding costs − Your fee

The classic "70% rule" is a starting point, not a finish line. Real deals require pulling actual comps, verifying repair estimates, and factoring in market velocity (days on market, competing offers, seasonal trends).
