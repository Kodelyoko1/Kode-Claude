How it works

Government data → weekly seller leads. Fully automated.

Step 1

We pull from public records

Tax delinquency rolls, deed transfer filings, probate dockets, and city code-violation lists — all free government data, zero list-broker fees passed to you.

Step 2

Agent emails owners for you

A 6-touch email sequence (day 1, 3, 7, 14, 21, 30) goes out in your name from your Gmail. Conversational tone, not spammy. Reply-to is your address.

Step 3

You get weekly deal reports

Every Monday a formatted report lands in your inbox: new prospects found, emails sent, who replied, hot leads flagged. You decide who to call.

Step 4

You call the hot leads

Hot leads are sellers who replied to the email sequence. They raised their hand — now you call and make an offer. Close rate on warm replies is significantly higher than cold calls.

Data sources

Where the leads come from.

No middlemen, no list fees, no scraped skip-tracing databases that everyone else is using.

🏛

Tax Delinquency Rolls

County and city open-data portals list every property with past-due taxes. Owners behind on taxes are often behind on everything — highly motivated to sell.

📄

Deed Transfer Records

Recent inherited properties, out-of-state owners, and inter-family transfers show up in deed filings. Heirs who don't want to manage property are prime wholesale targets.

🔩

Code Violation Logs

Code-enforcement databases list properties cited for violations — vacant structures, overgrown lots, structural issues. Owners of problem properties often want out.

⚖

Probate Filings

When a property owner dies, probate becomes public record. Heirs who inherited a house they don't want — and can't maintain — are some of the most motivated sellers you'll ever find.

FAQ

Honest answers.

What markets do you cover?

Any market with publicly available digital records — which includes most U.S. cities and counties. Before you pay, we’ll confirm we can pull records for your target markets. If we can’t, you don’t pay. Some rural counties with paper-only records may not work.

How many leads will I get per week?

Depends heavily on market size and data density. A large city like Detroit or Memphis typically yields 15–40 new prospects/week from a single market. Smaller markets may yield 5–15. Quality matters more than volume — tax-delinquent probate leads have high motivation.

Whose email does the outreach come from?

From your email address. You provide a Gmail account and app password (takes 5 minutes). Replies come directly to you. Our agent sends using your credentials — sellers respond to you, not us.

Is this CAN-SPAM compliant?

Yes. The agent includes an unsubscribe mechanism in every sequence, uses accurate subject lines, and includes your physical business address (or a registered agent address if you prefer). It also suppresses unsubscribes on future runs.

What do I need to provide to get started?

After payment: your target market(s), a Gmail address for outreach, a quick buy-box summary, and whether you want us to use your name or a business name in the emails. Setup takes about 24 hours from your first payment.

Can I cancel mid-month?

Yes. Cancel via PayPal anytime. We don’t prorate or charge cancellation fees. Your campaign runs through the last paid day and then stops.

What if I want more than 4 markets?

Reach out — we handle custom volume arrangements. Pricing scales roughly linearly above 4 markets (so 8 markets would be around $1,600/mo).
