FAQ

Common questions

How many buyers can I expect per month?
Varies by market. Active metros like Portland ME or Manchester NH typically yield 10–30 new contacts per weekly run. Smaller markets may average 5–15. After 3 months most operators have 50–100 active buyer contacts — the minimum needed to move deals consistently.
Is scraping REIA sites legal?
REIA member directories are publicly visible to encourage networking between investors. The agent accesses only public-facing pages — no login bypass, no rate-limit circumvention. It mimics a normal browser read of a publicly listed directory. Hotfrog is explicitly designed for business discovery and allows scraping for non-commercial use consistent with normal browsing.
What email does it send?
A short, conversational intro: who you are, what market you're active in, and what deal criteria you're looking for. No mass-blast copy — it reads like a direct investor-to-investor email. You can review and customize the template in buyer_finder/tools.py before the first run.
Does it work with the Deal Analyzer?
Yes. Once buyers are in data/cash_buyers.json, the Deal Analyzer agent can filter them by criteria (fix & flip, buy & hold, multi-family) when drafting LOIs — so you're always matching the right deal to the right buyer in your list.
