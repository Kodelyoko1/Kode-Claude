What It Does

4-Stage ffmpeg Cleanup Chain

Every episode runs the same professional-grade chain — no settings to fiddle with.

✂️

Silence Removal

Dead air and long pauses stripped automatically using ffmpeg silenceremove. Typical episode loses 3–8% dead time — tighter listening experience.

🔊

Room Noise Filter

Highpass at 80 Hz removes low-frequency rumble (AC units, traffic). Keeps voice frequencies crisp without affecting speech intelligibility.

📊

Dynamic Compression

2:1 acompressor evens out volume swings between guests and hosts — no more listener riding the volume knob through your interviews.

📻

EBU R128 Loudness

Normalized to -16 LUFS / -1.5 TP — the broadcast standard for Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and YouTube. Output is a clean 192k MP3 ready to publish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any audio software installed?

Only ffmpeg, which is free and runs on Linux, macOS, and Windows. The agent handles the ffmpeg command chain automatically — you never write any commands yourself.

Will this work on interview recordings with multiple speakers?

Yes. The compression stage is especially effective on multi-speaker recordings with volume disparities between guests. The highpass and silence removal work on the whole track regardless of speaker count.

What's in the .meta.json output?

Before/after durations, the percentage of silence removed, the measured loudness of the output (LUFS and true peak), and — if a matching Transcribe output exists — words per minute for the episode.

Can I adjust the cleanup parameters (e.g., silence threshold)?

Default settings work for 90%+ of podcasts. Custom thresholds (silence gate level, compression ratio, target LUFS) are available on the monthly and bulk plans — email WholesaleOmniverse@gmail.com with your requirements.
