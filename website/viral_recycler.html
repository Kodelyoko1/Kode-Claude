Common questions

Is this safe for my channel?

The agent applies heavy transformation (recut, mirror, recolor, new captions, branded intro/outro), credits the original creator, and caps posts at 2/day to avoid spam detection. Default safety mode only processes Creative Commons sources unless you explicitly allow others.

What do I need to set up?

One YouTube OAuth authorization (5 minutes, free). TikTok works via email handoff until your TikTok Business app is approved.

How does the free trial work?

30 days of full access. No credit card up front. Day 23 we email you a reminder. Day 30 the agent stops posting until you pay. Cancel anytime by ignoring the renewal.

Can I choose the niche?

Yes — Motivational, Comedy Skits, or Men’s Wellness. Each niche has its own hook templates, B-roll suggestions, and hashtag bank.
