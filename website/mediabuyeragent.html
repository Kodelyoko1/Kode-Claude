Features

Every Morning, Before You Wake Up

Runs on a daily cron. Defaults to dry-run — you review before it touches your account. Set MB_LIVE=1 to push actions automatically.

📊

Budget Reallocation

Reads 7-day performance by ad set. Winners above ROAS threshold get budget increases. Consistent losers get paused. Pure-function decision rules — no ML, fully auditable.

✍️

Claude Creative Generation

When a creative burns out (frequency ≥ 3.5 or CTR drops below baseline), the agent calls Claude to generate 3 new ad copy variants matched to your brand voice and target audience.

Conversions API (CAPI)

Server-side event tracking bypasses iOS 14+ signal loss. The e-commerce profile sends purchase and add-to-cart events directly to Meta from your server — accurate attribution even with ad blockers.

📧

Daily Performance Report

Plain-English email every morning: spend, leads/purchases, CPL/CPA, ROAS, and a bulleted list of every action taken overnight with the reason. Audit log also written to JSONL for history.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the agent spend my money automatically?

No — by default it runs in dry-run mode and emails you a list of planned actions every morning. You review the report and reply to approve. Set MB_LIVE=1 in your environment only when you want actions to push directly to Meta without approval.

What Meta permissions do you need?

A System User token or a 60-day OAuth token with ads_management and business_management permissions on your Meta Business Suite account. We walk through the setup on the onboarding call.

How is this different from hiring a media buyer?

A human media buyer charges $2,000–$5,000/mo plus a percentage of ad spend, works 9-to-5, and makes subjective decisions. The Media Buyer agent runs 24/7, applies consistent pure-function rules, logs every decision, and costs a fraction of a freelancer — with no percentage-of-spend markup.

What ad spend budget do I need?

Minimum $30/day ($900/mo) to generate enough data for the optimization rules to be meaningful. Most clients run $100–$500/day. The agent is designed to be profitable at this spend level; if your ad spend is under $30/day, a manual human review is likely more cost-effective.
