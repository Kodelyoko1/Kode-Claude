FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to supply a transcript?
Yes — paste or upload the transcript from your long-form video (podcast, interview, YouTube video, voice memo). ShortsForge reads the text, identifies the highest-value 30–60 second window, and builds the entire brief around it. Raw transcripts with timestamps work best, but plain text is fine too.
What is a "storyboard" in this context?
The storyboard (Unlimited plan) is a frame-by-frame breakdown of the Short: what appears on screen at each second, when B-roll should cut in, where text overlays appear, and what the caption says per frame. It is a written document your editor can follow like a shot list — no design tool or visual template required.
Which niche performs best on Shorts?
All three niches have strong Shorts audiences, but performance depends on consistency and posting cadence more than niche selection. Motivational tends to get the fastest early velocity from saves and shares; Men's Wellness compounds well over time from search; Comedy relies most heavily on the hook landing in the first two seconds. ShortsForge optimizes the brief for each niche's specific retention patterns.
Can I use this for TikTok or Reels too?
Yes. The hook formula, trim plan, and caption strategy translate directly to TikTok and Instagram Reels — all three platforms use the same vertical 9:16 format and favor the same 30–60 second retention curve. The SEO pack includes hashtags tuned for YouTube Shorts, but you can swap them for TikTok or Reels equivalents in under a minute using the niche and keyword notes in the brief.
