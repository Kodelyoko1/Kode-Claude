Common questions

How fast do offers come in?

Most listed properties get the first inquiry within 24–72 hours of going on the list. Pre-foreclosure and distressed properties tend to get more attention faster.

Do I have to fix anything?

No. Cash buyers expect as-is. The worse the condition, the better the fit usually — investors look specifically for distressed properties.

What does listing cost me?

Nothing. Zero. We make money from investor subscriptions, not seller fees. There will never be a charge or commission to a seller.

Will I be pressured to sell?

No. You're free to accept any offer, counter, or pass on everything. We're not in the contract — you negotiate directly with the buyer.
